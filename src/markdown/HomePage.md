## Home Page

Tired of bad traffic on Highway 97? Looking for a more convenient way to get to Kelowna's hospital or airport? We can help!

Our mission is to bring passenger rail to the Thompson-Okanagan region, from Kamloops to Kelowna to Osoyoos. Right now, people need more options to get across the valley, and the more people take the train, the less cars there are on Highway 97 during your daily commute!

There are other benefits, too—things like better air quality, fewer car accidents, not having to stress about finding parking, and so much more. We also think it'll be more cost efficient in the long run compared to blasting through bedrock to widen the highway a few years from now.
